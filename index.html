Meet the dj

DJ Piggel is a vinyl collector, digger and DJ. He plays all kinds of music (as long as it’s available on vinyl & you can dance on it) with a love for Funk, Soul, Rare Groove, Hip-Hop, Reggae & Ska. Resident DJ at Bibelot & Brandstof.

And together with DJ AYDQ -> The Funk Soul Brothers.
